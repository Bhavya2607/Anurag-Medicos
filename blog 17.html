Depression : Causes, Symptoms, Treatments:

Depression is a mood disorder that can affect a person's daily life.
It may be described as feelings of sadness, loss, or anger .

Depression is a feeling of sadness and loss of intrest, getting irritated
anger. It can also effect the mental health of a person. Here are the causes

History
Depression was initially called "melancholia". The earliest accounts of melancholia
appeared in ancient Mesopotamian texts in the second millennium B.C. At this time, all
mental illnesses were thought to be caused when someone was taken over by demons (possession)
They were then treated by priests.
