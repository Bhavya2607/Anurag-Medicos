Protein Powders : Top-5, Side Effects, Dosage

Protein Powder are proteins in powdered form which comes from plants
(rice, potatoes, soyabean, peas), eggs, milk. These also include other
material such as artificial flavouring, sugar , vitamins, minerals etc.

Protein Powder is a popular nutritional supplement. Protein is an
essential macronutrient that helps build muscle, repair tissue, and
make enzymes and hormones. Using protein powder may also aid weight loss
and help people tone their muscles. If these help us this much and has a
lot of benefits then it would surely have side effects.

Side Effects
 1)  High Blood Sugar Level
2)  Weight Increment
3)  Tiredness

History
In the 1930s, a pharmacist called Eugene Schiff developed a method of processing whey
from milk for human consumption. Schiff created ‘Schiff Bio-Foods’, a company selling
what was effectively protein isolate. Confined to drug stores, the product did not
receive much commercial success.
