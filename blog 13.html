Migrane : Causes, Symptoms, Treatments:

A migrane is a headache that causes severe pain at 1 side of head
It feels like dreadfull and there can be treat only by an medical
professional. It makes person sensitive of light and sound .

Cause:

1)  Drinks
Usually drinks that includes alcohol, especially wine, and too much caffine
i.e Coffee.

2)  Stress
 Stress of work can cause migrane.

3)  Light
 Bright flash light can trigger migrane in some peoples

4)  Sound
 Loud Sounds can also be an reason.

5)  Fragrances
 Paint Thinner, Perfumes. Smoke can also trigger

6)  Wheather Changes

7)  Sleep Chnages

8)  Medication

9)  Aged Food
Aged Food like Chesse, salt can also cause migrane.

These are some predictive causes but till now real cause are not known till now

Symptoms:
1)  Headaches in one side

2)  Vomiting

3)  Sensitive to Light, Sound, Fragrances

4)  Vision Loss

5)  Difficult Speaking

6)  Constipation

7)  Frequent Yawning

8)  Increased Urination

9)  Neck Stiffness

10)  Mood Changes

There are several other symptoms that are notice in people before migrane
