Constipation: Symptoms, Causes, Treatments.

Constipation is when a person passes less than three bowel movements
a week or has difficult bowel movements. Its destroys full day of
of a person and is very irritating. Here are its causes.

Constipations are very common and can be caused to anyone.
Here are its causes.

Causes
 1)  Blocked Rectum
May slowdown or stop bowls movement

2)  Hormone Balance
Diseases like Diabetes, Pregnancy can upset the hormone balance.

3)  Eating Food low in Fiber

4)  Not Drinking Enough Water

5)  Not Exercising

6)  Stress

7)  Eating enough milk and cheese

8)  Routine Changes

There several other cause that can cause constipation and
some of them may be still not known



Here are the symptoms of constipation.

Symptoms:
 1)  Hard to pass stools
2)  Stools are hard, dry
3)  Stomach Ache
4)  Feeling Heavy or Bloated

Contact Doctor if :
 1)  When Its since 2-3 weeks
2)  When you get it oftenly
3)  when you have any other disease.

about how health care providers should respond to constipation in patients
