Anulom Vilom And Its Benefits.

Anulom Vilom is the best meditation position for enough oxygen supply.It supplies
enough oxygen and take out waste carbon die oxide. It good blood flow also.
It is a great method to boost up your mind and body. It maintains the oxygen
level our body
.

Steps to do it.

1) Sit on the ground with cross legs

2) Hold you 1 nostril with thumb and inhale with open nostril as deep as you can.

3) Hold on the air in for 10 seconds do not force much.

4) Now put you index finger on second nostril and remove thumb from 1st nostril
and exhale with the 1st nostril.

5) Again repeat this process ( Inhale with 1st nostril and exhale with 2nd nostril )

6) Repeat this process 15-20 time early morning in a day
