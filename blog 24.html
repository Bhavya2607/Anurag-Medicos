How To Get Rid Of Dandruff: 8 Proven and Successful Ways

Dandruff is a common condition when skin of scalp causes flake. It feels embarrassing
and reduces our confidence which is not good. It isn't a serious problem but can be
difficult to treat permanently.


What is dandruff?
Dandruff is actually an accumulation of dead skin cells along with oil and dirt. It leads to
itching of scalp or itches on neck and elsewhere. Hair also gets covered in these flakes of
dandruff. Some of the signs of dandruff are flaky skin around the scalp and flakey white flakes.
Causes of dandruff Dandruff is one of the three types of skin condition and other two being
acne and psoriasis. Dandruff is caused by clogged pores, bacteria or fungi which get collected
around your scalp. It can be cured by a good shampoo or even by soap and water. But if a person
doesn't get treated properly, it might take years to treat it completely. But we give you a few
natural tips that you can try to remove dandruff. You need to follow these three ways to treat
dandruff.




Ways to get rid of dandruff.

1)  Wash Your Hair Less
he frequent shampooing of the hair is one of the major causes of dandruff. It can also
worsen the condition. Try not to use shampoo every day. If possible, use a clean
conditioner and lather it in your hair. Avoid washing hair too much, especially
before sleeping.

2)  Do Not Use the Compressor
If you don't wash your hair frequently, the air inside the tank of the machine will have
a lot of dirt and dust which may affect your scalp and worsen your dandruff. If you use a
humidifier or dehumidifier to keep the temperature at night, that causes excessive sweating
which can cause dandruff to worsen

3)  Use A Warm Washer
Using a warm washer will make your hair soft and moisturized. If you dry your hair using a
cold dryer, the hair may be dry and frizzy.
