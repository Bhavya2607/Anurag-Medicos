Eye Floaters : Causes, Symptoms, Treatments

Eye Floaters are the black or grey lines or dots that appear
in your vision. They slide or move far as you try to see them.
These are very irritating and can be caused to anyone

Eye floaters are caused when a jelly like substance named Viterous
more liquid than usual. Very tiny fibres in your viterous tend to
clump and projects tiny shadows on your retina. These shadows are
that are seen are called floaters.

Causes
 1)  Ageing Changes
Ageing can be one of the most biggest factor of eye floaters
As you age the viterous that provide shape to eye becomes
more liquid and as it will shrink fibers in it will clump
and result shadow on retina which is seen as floaters

2)  Inflammation in Eye
An inflammation in back eye is called Posterior uveitis
This condition can cause the release of inflammatory debris
into the vitreous that are seen as floaters. Posterior uveitis
may be caused by infection, inflammatory diseases or other causes.

3)  Bleeding in Eye
Bleeding in eye can be due to several reasons like hypertension
diabetes, blocked blood vessel, injury. They block cells and
that blocked cells are seen as eye floaters.

4)  Torn Retina
A torn retina is a serious problem that makes your vision blurry.
It is when the retina has a tear or hole, like a rip in cloth. A torn
retina often leads to a more serious condition called a detached retina.
This is where the retina is lifted away from the back of the eye.

5)  Meditations or surgeries
Certain surgeries or meditation into the viterous could form
air or oil bubbles between the jelly and can cause eye floaters



There can be also several other reason for eye floaters.

Symptoms:
 1)  Small black or grey shapes in your vision
2)  Transperant string or floating materialin your vision.
3)  Most noticeable when see on a plain background like blue sky
3)  Small shapes that drift out when trying to see

Contact Doctor if :
 1)  You notice many more floaters that usual
2)  Suddenly notice new floaters
3)  Flashes of light in the same eye as the floaters
3)  Darkness on vision.















Treatment
 1)  Ignore Them
If you notice 1-2 floaters ignore them, they will
settle down due to gravity and won't be visible

2)  Laser Therapy
You can contact your doctor if noticed eye
floaters suddenly. In this specialist aim eye
floater with laser that destroys the clump

3)  Vitrectomy
It is a surgery performed by specialist
In this specialist with remove viterous of your
eye with incision and clear it.

Home Remedy

Pine has many enzymes that may decompose the
extracellular substances that cause microscopic fibers to form.

History
The perception of these floating apparitions, sometimes also called vitreous
floaters or volitantes (Latin for “flying flies”), is known as myodesopsia.
They may appear as spots, small threads, filaments, or cobwebs and they're not
optical illusions. They're really there, drifting about inside your eyes
