Can We Take COVID-19 Vaccine If We Have Any Diseases?

Nowdays COVID-19 has spread a lot and almost 40% people
have have been infected with it once.
So to prevent it some measures are to be taken like
washing hands and wearing mask and maintaing social distancing
and one main prevention can be getting vaccinated. But can
we take vaccine if we have any disease ?

Vaccine develops antibodies in our body help help us to fight with covid-19
But is it safe to take vaccine if we have any diseases?

Yes, It is safe to take covid vaccine if you have the following diseases:

1)Common Cold
2)Fever (if negative from COVID-19)
3)Body Pain
4)Any cut or wound
5)any normal skin allergy
6)Infections
7)Diabetes
8)Blood Pressure

These are some normal diseases but you should consult your doctor in severe
disease like:

1)Malaria
2)Diarrhea
3)Typhoid

DONOT TAKE VACCINE WITHIN 3 MONTHS IF YOU HAVE CURED FROM COVID-19 .
