Hair Fall : Causes, Treatment

Hair fall also known as alopecia or baldness is a condition
when hairs fall due to certain reason from head .

Everyone loves his/her hairs and even many of us care them
a lot. but after care do you notice hairfall here are the cause
of hairfall.

Cause:

 1)  Herediatry
Herediatry is a very common cause of hairfall which is also
called androgenic alopecia. In men a receding hairline and
bald spots and thinning hair along the crown of the scalp in women.

2)  Hormonal Changes
Conditions like pregnancy, childbirth, menopause and tyroid
problems can cause hairfall

3)  Medical conditions
A immune system related problem named alopecia areata cause
patchy hair loss, scalp infections such as ringworm, and a
hair-pulling disorder called trichotillomania

4)  Medications and supplements
Hairfall can be caused by side feects of certain drugs
or medicines of cancer, arthisis, depression, high/low
blood pressure, heart problems etc.

5)  Stress
Hair Thinning is noticed after a months of sudden shock or stress.

6)  HairStyling and Treatments
Hairstyling or colouring of hair several times can cause a permanant
hair damage . Treatments like Hot oil massage can also stop growing of
new hair. There can be several other causes of hairfall and here are the symptoms of it.



Symptoms:

 1)  Thinning hair on top centre of head
This is very common and effect people as they age

2)  Circular or patchy bald spots
Some people loses hair in pattern of circular patches on scalp,
beard, eyebrows. The skin may become itchy or painful before
the hair falls out.

3)  Suddenlossing of hair
Stress or emotional shock can cause sudden lossing of hair

4)  Full Body Hair Loss
Medical conditions like cancer can cause full body hair loss

There are several other symptoms that are notice in Hairfall





Treatments

 1)Be Gentle with your hair do not pull them and wash them regularly.

2)Do not use different type of shampoos and conditioners

3)Do not apply harmfull colours or do several treatments for hair

4)Apply oil atleast twice a week

5)Protect your hair from sunlight and ultravoilate rays

6)Stop Smoking

7)If you're being treated with chemotherapy, ask your doctor about
a cooling cap. This cap can reduce your risk of losing hair during
chemotherapy.
